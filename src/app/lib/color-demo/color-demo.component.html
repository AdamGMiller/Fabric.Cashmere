Color

Following are the suggested colors for use within your application. Some expansion beyond the general palette is acceptable as long as the additional colors are similar in value/saturation. It's reccommended that you import the colors and leverage the sass variables.

Primary Palette

The primary colors used for branding, calls to action (buttons, links, etc), and other accents.

Neutral Palette

The colors used for backgrounds, text, etc.

Secondary Palette

Additional options for accent colors.

Stratifying and Adding Colors

There may be times that a product may either need a variation of a color in either in saturation or value, or even still just need a more expansive color pallet. In these cases we recommend using coolors.co to stratify the base colors spelled out on the previous page or to generate colors while keeping close to the same color scheme. This color tool will also help you determine you should use dark or light on top of your color.

How to Use

        
        @import "../colors";

        .my-component {
            background-color: $slate-gray-100;
        }
